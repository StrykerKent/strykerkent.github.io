What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the attempt to increase traffic to your website by ranking near the top on search engine result pages. There are multiple factors that affect SEO including site structure, content, keyword competition, relevant sites that link to your site, and site load time. Search engines win when they match users with the right website. Websites win when they rank high for the right keywords.

SEO is always changing. It requires constant research to stay up with the latest rules and tactics.



What is Local SEO?

Local SEO is marketing your business online by geographic area. When you search on google, have you seen a list of two to three businesses with a map (pictured right) ?
