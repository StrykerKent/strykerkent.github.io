A Little About Me...



I am the son of an engineer, which explains where I get my problem solving skills from. I enjoy programming and analyzing data. I find myself doing it even when I am not at work (like right now).


My Path


Over 15 years ago, I started building websites in High School in a "web mastering" class (the basics... HTML, CSS, JavaScript). I loved it. Went on to college, took more programming, database, web design (Photoshop, Illustrator, and Flash) and web development classes. Built a website for my college Ultimate Frisbee Team.

Graduated, acquired a job as a Web Developer at a website company (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL). It didn't pay much (this was a long time ago) so I moved on to the Oil Industry and created spreadsheets, databases, reports, web applications, and websites (internal and external). I also analyzed Sales and Business Data for C-level executives, the Sales & Marketing Department, and the Estimating Department.

This is where I discovered automating processes with programming in Excel and Access using VBA, VB Script, and PowerShell for scripting tasks on my computer; SQL, Python, QlikView, and Microsoft Power BI for Data Analysis and Reporting; and ASP.NET MVC C# for Web Applications. That is where I am at today. I subscribe to a couple of learning websites, so I can keep up to date and learn new skills, languages, concepts, and computer programs.
